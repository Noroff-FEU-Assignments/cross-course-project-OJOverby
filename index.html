You should adopt a cybersecurity framework, with research on security risks and an incident response plan for problems that can occur.

By automate and integrate security tools such as vulnerability trackers and issue trackers you remove a lot of manual labor and remove a lot of human error. Problems can be detected and dealt with early in the development process.
